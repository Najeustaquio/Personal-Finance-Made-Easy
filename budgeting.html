Key items to consider when defining and monitoring a household budget:

  • Track all sources of income
  • List fixed expenses (rent, utilities, insurance)
  • Estimate variable expenses (groceries, entertainment)
  • Set savings goals
  • Allocate funds for debt repayment
  • Review and adjust monthly
